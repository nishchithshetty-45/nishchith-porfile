Adaptive Marketing India · Jun 2023 – Present

In 2022, I was doing everything manually. Scraping leads one by one, sending emails one at a time, following up the same way. It worked — but it was exhausting, inconsistent, and completely unscalable.

Then I discovered automation. And everything changed.

I started building workflows with n8n, Clay, Instantly, and SuperAGI — connecting lead enrichment → multi-channel sequencing → AI-personalized outreach → follow-ups → reporting. What used to take a team of 5 now runs while I sleep.

Today, I help GTM teams replace chaos with precision. Every system I build has one job: make the next reply come easier.
